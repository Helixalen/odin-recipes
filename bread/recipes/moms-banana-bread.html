Mom's Banana Bread

What is It???

This is my mom's banana bread recipe. I cannot live without it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large ripe bananas (the older the better)
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • Cinnamon-sugar mix

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  4. Mix in bananas, flour, salt, baking soda.
  5. Add nuts.
  6. Pour in greased pan(s) and bake for 50-60 minutes.
  7. While bread is still hot in pan, brush with melted butter and sprinkle cinnamon-sugar mix over.
  8. Remove from pan and immediately wrap in aluminum foil while still hot.
Makes one large loaf or two small ones.
